Our story

Where Paint Meets
Purpose


The journey of Creative Art Academy began with two passionate art enthusiasts from Bhiknoor Mr. Lakkapathni Bhagavan and Mr. Arupula Rahul, who studied in the same school and shared a deep interest in creativity and art from a young age.

Their passion led them to pursue higher education at Jawaharlal Nehru Architecture and Fine Arts University (JNAFAU), Hyderabad, one of the most prestigious institutions for art and design. Mr. Lakkapathni Bhagavan successfully completed his Post-Graduation at JNAFAU, while Mr. Arupula Rahul is currently pursuing his Undergraduate studies there.

With a shared vision to inspire and guide young creative minds, they founded Creative Art Academy in Shaikpet, Hyderabad. Their goal is to create a space where students can explore their imagination, develop artistic skills, and build confidence in their creative journey.

At Creative Art Academy, we believe that every student has the potential to create something extraordinary all they need is the right guidance and inspiration.
